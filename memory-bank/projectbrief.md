# GoddessGPT UI - Project Brief

## Core Mission
Build a modern, empowering AI companion web application specifically designed for women seeking trusted guidance in healthcare, legal matters, and mental wellness.

## Project Goals
- **Accessibility**: Provide 24/7 access to AI specialists in critical life domains
- **Empowerment**: Give women tools and knowledge to make informed decisions
- **Safety**: Create a judgment-free, secure environment for sensitive conversations
- **Community**: Foster supportive connections between women with shared experiences
- **Trust**: Maintain professional standards while remaining approachable

## Target Users
- Women seeking medical guidance and health information
- Women needing legal support and rights awareness  
- Women looking for mental wellness and emotional support
- Women wanting to connect with a supportive community

## Core Features
1. **AI Chat Interface**: Three specialized AI assistants (Medical, Legal, Mental Health)
2. **Community Platform**: Safe space for sharing experiences and peer support
3. **Modern UI**: Glass morphism design with empowering aesthetics
4. **Privacy First**: End-to-end encryption and confidential conversations

## Success Metrics
- User engagement with AI specialists
- Active community participation
- User retention and trust indicators
- Positive feedback on guidance quality

## Non-Goals
- Replace professional medical/legal advice
- Become a social media platform
- Support male-focused use cases (though not explicitly restricted)
- Monetization through user data selling
